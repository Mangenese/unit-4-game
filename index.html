Rupee Collector!

At the start of the game you will be given a random number.

You have 4 crystals to choose from. By clicking on one of those buttons you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win if you can match your total score to the random number that was generated. You lose if you can't match your total to the random total.

The value of each total is kept hidden until you click on it.

Each time the game starts the crystals vaules will change!
